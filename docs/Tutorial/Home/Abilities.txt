====== Abilities ======
Created Saturday 25 June 2022


Dash Punch - Unblockable, unstaggerable

Evasive Maneuvers - Causes your lock target to lose track of you

Evade - A front-blocking back jump

Fading Horizon - A held draw attack, will lock on to nearest target while holding

High Jump - A jump with a height of 4

Leap - A forward leap that can be chained into a dash attack by left clicking during the leap

Lick - Unblockable attack aimed at head, currently not that useful

Pull - Pulls an enemy towards you and causes a brief stagger along the way

Quickturn - This ability is for turning quickly with ice skates

Spin Attack - Hit's up to 4 enemies around you

Summon Dummy - Summons a friendly target dummy, can resummon once that one is dead, pretty buggy and often spawns outside the map

Taunt - Gets the aggro of everything in your lockon zone

Throw - Throwing knives don't deal damage, but do get attention and cause a small stagger

Void Step - Teleport to a random nearby spot (while front blocking!)
